Our Legacy of Excellence

Since 1999, Echoes of Joy has been at the forefront of private education in Kenya, combining the best of British curriculum with Kenyan values.

Our mission is to provide a nurturing environment where students can achieve academic excellence while developing strong character, leadership skills, and social responsibility.

Why Choose Us?

  • 1:12 Teacher-Student Ratio
